Projects

SHARDS

I am a member of the vocal ensemble SHARDS. We're featured on a couple of new tracks, one called Sandham by Emmy award-winning composer Michael Price for his new album Tender Symmetry, the other Misty Avalanche by vibraphonist Masayoshi Fujita, for his new album Book of Life, both released on Erased Tapes.



^Video of the making of Sandham, at the beautiful Sandham Memorial Chapel. The interior is covered in paintings by Stanley Spencer, based on his experiences in WW1.
