Jack is a classically trained baritone, based in London. He studied at the Guildhall School of Music and Drama.

Jack performs in operas, concerts and recitals. He is a Britten Pears Young Artist, a London Festival Opera artist, a Garsington Alvarez Young Artist and a Live Music Now artist.
