Jack Lawrence-Jones, baritone, is a graduate of the Guildhall School of Music and Drama. He recently made his Aldeburgh Festival debut, as a Britten-Pears Young Artist Alumnus.

Jack is currently working with Garsington Opera as an Alvarez Young Artist. He is singing in the chorus for Mozart’s Die Zauberflöte and Verdi’s Falstaff, and playing the role of Servant 2 in Strauss’ Capriccio and covering the Count.
